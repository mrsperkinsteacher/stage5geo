Stage 5 Geography | Year 9

Changing Places

How do urbanisation and migration change the places we live?

About this unit. Despite the vague name, this topic is about two specific ways places change: urbanisation and migration. We investigate why the world is moving to the city, what happens inside megacities, why Australians live where they do, how Sydney is planning for growth, who moves within Australia (and China), and how people come to Australia from overseas. Along the way we build geography skills: choropleth maps, climate graphs, pie graphs, fieldwork and data analysis. Every page on this site is written so that a teacher who does not know the subject can pick up the lesson and teach it.
Big questions this term: Are all geographic changes negative? Who wins and who loses when places change? How do we steward the places we are given?
